Daily Health Routines

Your guide to a healthier lifestyle

Morning Routine

  • Wake Up Early – Aim for 6-8 hours of sleep
  • Hydrate – Drink a glass of water to kickstart metabolism
  • Stretch or Exercise – 15-30 minutes of stretching, yoga, or a workout
  • Healthy Breakfast – Include proteins, fiber, and healthy fats
  • Hygiene & Skincare – Brush, floss, and wash your face

Throughout the Day

  • Stay Hydrated – Drink at least 2-3 liters of water
  • Balanced Meals – Incorporate proteins, whole grains, fruits, and vegetables
  • Take Breaks – Avoid prolonged sitting; move every 30-60 minutes
  • Practice Mindfulness – Take 5-10 minutes to meditate or breathe deeply
  • Limit Caffeine & Sugar – Excessive intake can lead to energy crashes

Evening/Night Routine

  • Light Dinner – Eat 2-3 hours before bedtime; avoid heavy meals
  • Reduce Screen Time – Limit blue light exposure an hour before sleep
  • Practice Gratitude – Reflect on the day and set intentions
  • Hygiene Routine – Brush, floss, and wash your face
  • Sleep on Time – Aim for a consistent sleep schedule
